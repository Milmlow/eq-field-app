Logging In
1. Go to sks-nsw-labour.netlify.app on your phone or computer
2. Type your name in the search field — tap your name when it appears
3. Enter the 4-digit staff access code and tap Enter
4. Tick "Remember me" to stay logged in for 24 hours
Checking Your Schedule
1. Your schedule loads automatically after logging in
2. Each day shows the site name, address, and person in charge
3. Tap the arrow icon to open the site address in Google Maps
4. Use the week arrows at the top to look at other weeks
Submitting Leave
1. Tap Leave in the menu
2. Tap + New Leave Request
3. Select your name, leave type, and dates
4. Choose a supervisor as the approver
5. Submit — you'll get an email when it's approved or rejected
Job Numbers
1. Tap Job Numbers in the menu
2. Search by job number or project name
3. Use this number when filling in your timesheet
Questions? Contact your supervisor or Royce Milmlow — 0432 944 014
Supervisor Login
Enter the supervisor code (not the staff PIN) to unlock editing. You'll land on the Dashboard with full access to edit rosters, manage people, approve leave, and view timesheets.
Editing the Roster
Edit Roster in the side menu. Click a cell, type the site abbreviation (e.g. STG, SY7, PORT). Use Copy Last Week to prefill or Batch Fill to assign a whole team at once. Changes save automatically.
Managing People & Sites
Add Person — name, phone, group, licence, agency, email. Duplicate names are blocked.
Sites — name, abbreviation, address, person in charge. Abbreviations must be unique.
Set emails on staff profiles so they receive leave approval notifications.
Approving Leave
Pending requests appear on the Dashboard and Leave page. Tap Review, approve or reject with a note. Approved leave automatically writes to the roster. You cannot approve your own requests.
Timesheets & Job Numbers
Timesheets — enter job numbers and hours for apprentices and labour hire. Hours over 12 show a warning, over 24 is blocked.
Job Numbers — add project numbers so staff can look them up. Import/export via CSV.
Backup & Security
Full Backup — top of Import/Export page. Download regularly.
Audit Log — bottom-left sidebar. All actions logged with who and when.
Logout — bottom of sidebar. Clears session and remember-me token.
5 failed login attempts triggers a 15-minute lockout.

🔐 Privacy Notice

SKS Technologies Pty Ltd
ABN 51 168 906 956  |  NSW Operations  |  Employee Privacy Notice v1.0
This notice explains how SKS Technologies collects, stores and uses your personal information in connection with our internal workforce management system.

1. What Information We Store

In this system we hold your name, mobile number, group (Direct/Apprentice/Labour Hire), licence type, agency (if applicable), weekly site allocations, and for Apprentices and Labour Hire, timesheet data (job numbers and hours per day).

2. Why We Hold It

Your information is used only for: weekly roster planning, providing you with your schedule and site addresses, enabling supervision to contact you, and timesheet/payroll reconciliation. It is not used for marketing and not shared with third parties for commercial purposes.

3. Who Can See It

Access LevelWhat They Can See / Do
Staff (access code)View roster, contacts, own schedule — read only
Supervision (password)Edit roster, enter timesheets, manage staff records

4. Where It Is Stored

Data is stored in a Supabase cloud database hosted in the ap-southeast-2 (Sydney) region. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. The application is hosted on Netlify with Asia-Pacific edge nodes.

5. How Long We Keep It

Roster and schedule data: rolling 12 months. Timesheet data: 7 years (Fair Work Act and tax obligations). Contact information: removed within 30 days of your departure from SKS Technologies.

6. Your Rights

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 you have the right to access, correct or request deletion of your personal information. Contact: royce.milmlow@sks.com.au or 0432 944 014.

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au or 1300 363 992.

7. Access Code

The staff access code for this system must not be shared outside SKS Technologies NSW. If you believe the code has been compromised, notify your supervisor immediately.
